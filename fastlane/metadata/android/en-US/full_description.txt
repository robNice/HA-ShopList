HA-ShopList is an Android app for managing Home Assistant shopping and todo lists on the go.

The app connects directly to your Home Assistant instance and lets you open and manage a selected list from your phone. Changes are synchronized in real time, so updates made in Home Assistant or on another device appear without manual refresh.

Features:
- connect to a Home Assistant instance
- use a selected shopping or todo list
- add, check, uncheck, rename, sort, and delete items
- receive live updates via Home Assistant WebSocket
- simple interface focused on fast list usage while away from home

The app stores the Home Assistant URL, access token, and selected list locally on the device in order to connect to your instance.
No external cloud service is used.
